# PRISM: Adaptive Invisible Shield
> **Tagline:** *Invisible. Intelligent. Unbroken.*

## Original Description
An invisible protective dome surrounding a person or object that can stop threats, selectively manage gases (such as retaining oxygen while filtering others), and protect against extreme environmental dangers.

## Core Vision
> *An intelligent envelope that filters out danger while preserving life.*

## Phased Building Procedures

### Phase 1: Active Threat Detection & Monitoring
- Integrate multi-spectral computer vision, millimeter-wave radar, and ultrasonic sensors for high-speed dynamic tracking of physical vectors.
- Implement environmental gas chromatography sensors to monitor real-time toxic PPM, oxygen, and carbon dioxide levels.

### Phase 2: Air Intelligence & Micro-Atmosphere Regulation
- Construct a pressurized breathing zone using compact high-efficiency particulate air (HEPA), carbon molecular sieves, and catalytic scrubbers.
- Implement active oxygen enrichers and adaptive intake/exhaust valves to selectively retain clean air while venting toxins.

### Phase 3: Tactical Physical Protection Boundaries
- **Kinetic Defense**: Deploy reactive compressed-air pulse deflection grids or micro-interceptor arrays to redirect incoming fast-moving debris.
- **Thermal/Radiation Isolation**: Utilize high-density localized aerogel curtains or electromagnetic plasma fields (experimental) to insulate the boundary from intense heat and radiation.

### Phase 4: Autonomous Survival Cocoon
- Orchestrate threat detection, atmospheric regulation, and impact barriers into an automated, portable bubble system that adapts instantaneously to environmental rupture.
