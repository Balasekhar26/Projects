# Kattappa North Star: The Three Eras

This document defines the milestone milestones across the three development Eras of Kattappa's cognitive timeline.

---

## Era I: Cognitive Foundation (Milestones K1 - K20)
Focuses on building the infrastructure of execution:
- Central Event, Memory, Goal, Context, and Tool Buses (`CognitiveKernel`).
- Structured working registers (`ExecutiveWorkspace`).
- Priority-ordered arbitration (`ConflictResolver`).
- Ethical guidance principles (`WisdomEngine`).
- SQLite-backed memory tiered consolidations.
- Initial Predictive Cognition Layer (beliefs and predictions).

## Era II: Cognitive Expansion (Milestones K21 - K50)
Focuses on building predictive cognition, models, and learning feedback:
- **K21**: World Model 2.0
- **K22**: Concept Formation Engine
- **K23**: Causal Reasoning Engine
- **K24**: Meta Reasoner
- **K25**: Curiosity Engine
- **K26**: Autonomous Experiment Engine
- **K27**: Dreaming / Offline Consolidation
- **K28**: Memory Replay
- **K29**: Self Improvement Engine
- **K30**: Self Code Evolution
- **K31 - K50**: Council orchestration, long-horizon planners, tool economies, and self-benchmarking.

## Era III: Open Research (Milestones K51+)
Focuses on open-ended capabilities driven by research hypotheses:
- Continual learning without catastrophic forgetting.
- Safe recursive self-improvement and formal verification.
- Distributed planning across clusters.
- Autonomous scientific discovery laboratories.
- Embodied physical models.
