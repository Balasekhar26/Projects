# Kattappa Scientific Philosophy

This document defines the core scientific philosophy that guides all research and engineering in the Kattappa cognitive program.

---

## 1. Ontological Definitions

*   **Intelligence**: The capacity of an autonomous agent to minimize its prediction error and optimize goal expected utilities under resource constraints.
*   **Knowledge**: A structured representation of the environment (physical, digital, social, self) that generates verifiable predictions.
*   **Truth**: The limit where prediction error approaches zero ($\lim E_{pred} \rightarrow 0$) over infinite observation samples.
*   **Evidence**: Observed outcomes that adjust belief confidence levels according to Bayesian or probabilistic update rules.
*   **Belief**: A transient state representation expressing the probability of an assertion.
*   **Self**: The system's internal model of its own capabilities, limitations, processes, and active resources.
*   **Agency**: The capacity of the system to generate plans, select tools, and execute actions to modify the environment in pursuit of goals.
