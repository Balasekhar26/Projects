# PORTAL: Spacetime Shortcuts & Distance Collapse
> **Tagline:** *Distance, undone.*

## Original Description
A system for instant relocation across distance through spacetime shortcuts, ideally using stable wormhole-like mechanisms to move the original matter itself.

## Core Vision
> *Bypassing physical travel by stitching coordinates together.*

## Phased Building Procedures

### Phase 1: Spacetime Geometry Research
- Map mathematical structures of Einstein-Rosen bridges (wormholes).
- Investigate the properties of exotic matter with negative energy densities required to keep wormhole throats stable and traversable.

### Phase 2: Quantum Entanglement & Entangled Spacetime (ER=EPR)
- Research the ER=EPR conjecture (wormholes are quantum entangled particles at a macro scale).
- Conduct micro-scale quantum teleportation experiments using state transfer across entangled channels.

### Phase 3: Coordinate Navigation & Motion Correction
- Design spatial targeting systems that map source coordinates to destination coordinates.
- Implement physical drift calculations (compensating for Earth's rotation, orbital speed, and altitude differences between exit/entry zones).

### Phase 4: Matter Stability Safeguards
- Engineer physical containment zones that prevent tidal force disruption, keeping biological and mechanical matter fully intact during shortcut transition.
